How it all began

Our Story

From a chance encounter to forever — every moment led us here.

March 2019

We Met

A rainy Saturday afternoon at a friend's housewarming party in Surry Hills. Emma spilled her drink, James offered a napkin, and the conversation never stopped.

April 2019

First Date

Brunch at a tiny café in Newtown that neither of us can remember the name of — but we both remember it lasted five hours and ended with a walk through Camperdown Memorial Park.

June 2021

Moved In Together

A little terrace in Paddington with a garden just big enough for two chairs and a lemon tree. It felt like home the moment we unpacked the first box.

December 2024

The Proposal

At sunset on a quiet beach in Byron Bay, James got down on one knee. Emma said yes before he even finished asking. There were tears, laughter, and a bottle of champagne shared under the stars.

September 2025

The Wedding

And now, we can't wait to celebrate this next chapter with the people we love most.

Travel & Accommodation

A few recommendations to make your visit comfortable and easy.

3 min drive · Chippendale

A boutique heritage hotel in the heart of Chippendale, with rooftop pool and award-winning restaurant. Perfect for making a weekend of it.

8 min drive · Surry Hills

Compact luxury meets clever design in vibrant Surry Hills. Walking distance to cafés, galleries, and some of Sydney's best dining.
